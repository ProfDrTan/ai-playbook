Backup / Off-Ramps
IF consulting inbound is slow

Lean harder into published content before assuming the service needs repositioning — visibility usually lags content investment by months.

IF guest lectures don't convert to repeat/paid teaching

Sample-syllabus-module content still has standalone value — could become a licensed micro-course.

IF China/Japan/Korea prove too heavy an on-ramp

Treat SEA (Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, Cambodia) as the durable core market; don't force NE Asia expansion on this timeline.

IF JCU relationship changes, better or worse

Architecture is designed to be JCU-independent — no rework needed either way.

IF the Pte Ltd creates friction with JCU's outside-activity policy

Teach and Publish pillars could stand alone without the Advise entity attached, though this weakens the "practitioner who teaches" positioning.
