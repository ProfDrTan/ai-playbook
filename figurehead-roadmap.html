03

Regional Activation

Aug 2027 – Jan 2028

Checkpoint: 24 Months (by ~Jul 2028)
04

Scale & Diversify

Feb – Jul 2028
Not a default "grow everything" phase — a decision point, made with 18 months of real data instead of assumptions.

Advise

  • Decide, using actual data from months 1–18: does Advise justify bringing on associate help, or stay solo/boutique by design?
  • If scaling: define what stays yours (strategy, figurehead-level relationships) vs. what an associate could deliver (Diagnostic-tier execution)
  • If not scaling: that's a legitimate outcome — a smaller, higher-margin solo practice may suit the "not zero effort, but not full-time" goal better than growth for its own sake

Teach

  • By now you'll know whether Teach or Advise shows the stronger inbound pull (per the month-18 gate) — weight your calendar accordingly rather than splitting evenly by default
  • If a formal visiting-faculty role has materialized, decide how it sits alongside (or replaces) the JCU part-time arrangement

Publish

  • Revisit AI Power Shift as a paid Signal Watch subscription tier — only if audience size actually supports it (check analytics, don't assume)
  • Consider whether a second book or major publication is warranted based on how much the first Sprint by Sprint paper actually moved credibility/inbound — measure before committing another 12+ months to a second one

Entity & Ops

  • Full review of entity structure — is Pte Ltd still the right vehicle, or does growth (or lack of it) suggest a different structure?
  • Formal year-2 financial review: revenue vs. cost of running the entity (accounting, filing, travel) — sanity-check that the venture is net-positive before extending further

Backup / Off-Ramps
IF consulting inbound is slow

Lean harder into published content before assuming the service needs repositioning — visibility usually lags content investment by months, not weeks.

IF guest lectures don't convert to repeat/paid teaching

Sample-syllabus-module content still has standalone value — could become a licensed micro-course.

IF China/Japan/Korea prove too heavy an on-ramp

Treat SEA (Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, Cambodia) as the durable core market; don't force NE Asia expansion on this timeline.

IF JCU relationship changes, better or worse

Architecture is designed to be JCU-independent — no rework needed either way.

IF the Pte Ltd creates friction with JCU's outside-activity policy

Teach and Publish pillars could stand alone without the Advise entity attached, though this weakens the "practitioner who teaches" positioning.

IF solo capacity becomes the bottleneck before month 18

Resist hiring reactively under client pressure — raise Diagnostic-tier pricing first to filter demand, then consider associate help only once the higher price point still clears.
