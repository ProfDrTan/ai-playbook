The one rule from TR2 worth keeping permanently, plus the draft schedule it produces. Details still to be flushed out as TR2 closes out — this page will be revisited before TR3 begins.

🔧 Draft — revisit after TR2 ends

The Convention (Locked In)

Sprint N = Week N of teaching. Always. No exceptions, no offsets.
This is the single fix for everything that went wrong in TR2: a tag numbering system (ISO calendar weeks) that quietly diverged from the sprint numbers everyone was actually reading and teaching from.
Tags are plain sequential — vW1, vW2, vW3... — not tied to the real calendar week of the year. No ISO week math anywhere in the system. The tag number and the sprint number are the same number, permanently.
The Study Week gets no sprint number at all — it's skipped silently in the sequence rather than being a numbered-but-blank slot. A numbered gap was part of what caused confusion last time.
The final numbered sprint is the last one before Demo Day — not Demo Day itself. Demo Day is the following Monday, untagged, no new prediction that week.
